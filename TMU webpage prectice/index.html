BTECH COMPUTER SCIENCE & ENGINEERING


Discover how the BTech Computer Science & Engineering program at TMU can shape your future and empower you to make a meaningful impact in your chosen field.


BTech in Computer Science and Engineering is a comprehensive four-year undergraduate engineering programme that focuses on providing students with the essential skills and knowledge in computer systems, software development, and related technologies. The programme is spread across eight semesters and covers various topics, from foundational computer science to advanced subjects like artificial intelligence and machine learning.


To be eligible for the BTech (Computer Science & Engineering) programme, candidates must have completed their 10+2 grade education with a minimum percentage of marks as required.


B.Tech (Computer Science & Engineering) programme Graduates can pursue careers in various industries, such as IT, software development, and data analysis, for multiple roles.
