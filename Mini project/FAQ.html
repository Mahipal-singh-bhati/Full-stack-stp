Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a tour package?

Visit our “Tour Packages” section, choose your preferred destination, and click “Book Now”. We will contact you shortly with details.

2. Do you provide hotel booking as well?

Yes, we offer hotel booking options from budget to luxury hotels, included in most tour packages.

3. Is transportation included in the package?

Transportation varies by package. Some include cabs, while others offer options for bike rentals.

4. Can I customize my trip?

Absolutely! You can choose custom destinations, travel dates, and services as per your needs.

5. What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations done 7 days before the trip start date are eligible for a 90% refund. After that, cancellation fees apply.
